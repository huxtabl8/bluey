Unofficial Bluey Character Guide

Here you can learn all about the hit animated children's show, Bluey! Follow the adventures of Bluey, Bingo, and their family as they play and learn valuable life lessons.

Bluey

Bluey is the main character of the show, a energetic and playful six-year-old Blue Heeler puppy. She is always finding new and creative ways to have fun with her family and friends.

Bingo

Bingo is Bluey's younger sister, a four-year-old Blue Heeler puppy with a big personality. She is always up for a good game or adventure with her family and loves to dress up and play pretend.

Chilli

Chilli is Bluey and Bingo's mother, a kind and patient Blue Heeler who works as a nurse. She always finds time to play and have fun with her family, and is always there to support and encourage them.

Bandit

Bandit is Bluey and Bingo's father, a playful and imaginative Blue Heeler who works as a firefighter. He loves to come up with fun games and activities for his family to enjoy, and is always there to support and protect them.
